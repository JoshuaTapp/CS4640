About Me

Who is Joshua Tapp?

Howdy Stranger, let me introduce myself.

Before my time at UVA I was a simple blue collar worker. I started my career as a entry level worker at Amazon's RDU5 sort center in Durham, NC. I quickly was promoted up and took a job as a Logistic Specialist in Richmond, VA. After two winters not seeing my family and wondering if this career track was what I wanted, I started taking classes at John Tyler Community College while I was working nights at Amazon.

years went by and I graduated in the top 1% of all students in 2020. I was accepted into the University of Virginia that same year and began my studies in computer science at the School of Engineering and Applied Science at UVA. My studies at UVA have been rewarding, with so many interesting subjects to learn. My dedication to my studies has not changed. As of Spring 2022, my GPA is 3.953 and will remain high until my graduation.
